MoldRemovalCleaner.com (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or request mold removal services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property/service address
  • Any details you provide when requesting a quote or contacting us

We may also collect non-personal information such as IP address, browser type, device type, and website usage data through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to service inquiries
  • Provide mold inspection and remediation services
  • Schedule appointments
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Communicate service updates

By submitting your information through our forms, you consent to receive phone calls, text messages, and emails related to your inquiry. Standard message and data rates may apply.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share your information with trusted contractors, technicians, service providers, or marketing partners strictly for the purpose of delivering mold removal services or responding to your request. All third parties are required to maintain confidentiality and protect your data.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and third-party analytics tools to improve performance, track website traffic, and enhance user experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, please contact us at: support@moldremovalcleaner.com
